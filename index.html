Privacy Policy for Nawa Chat

Last Updated: April 4, 2024

Note: This policy applies to the current beta version of Nawa Chat, which is under active development.

1. Summary

Nawa Chat is designed to prioritize your privacy. In its current beta stage, the app:

  • Supports 1:1 text chats only (no group chats, calls, or file sharing yet)
  • Does not collect, store, or share any personal data or usage information
  • Cannot access your messages, which are encrypted locally on your device

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

In this version of Nawa Chat:

  • No account required: You use the app anonymously without providing an email, phone number, or username
  • No message storage: Chats exist only on your device and the recipient's device. We have no servers to store them
  • No analytics or tracking: We don't use cookies, SDKs, or third-party tools to monitor your activity

3. Technical Safeguards

  • Local encryption: Messages are encrypted on your device before being sent to the recipient
  • No metadata: We don't log timestamps, IP addresses, or device identifiers
  • Open development: As we add features, we'll update this policy and prioritize privacy-first design

4. Your Control

  • Delete chats: Remove conversations permanently via the in-app delete button (erased from both devices)
  • No permissions required: The app doesn't request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, or location

5. Future Updates

As we expand features (e.g., group chats, file sharing), we will:

  • Update this policy clearly and in advance
  • Maintain a strict "no unnecessary data collection" rule
  • Continue using end-to-end encryption for new features
